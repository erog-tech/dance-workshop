About Us

G

Gela

Dance has always been my way to express emotions — it spoke for me when words couldn’t. Through movement, I feel alive. Rhythm, energy and expression became my language.

My foundation is Hip-Hop, which I blend with street, commercial and freestyle elements to create movement that’s authentic, emotional and powerful. Each choreography tells a story — it’s my way to connect with people through music.

In my classes, I focus on musicality, body awareness and building confidence. I want you to leave each session stronger, more present and more connected to the rhythm that moves you.

I believe dance is more than just steps — it’s energy, emotion, and how you feel when you move. I aim to create a space where you can push your limits, explore yourself and express who you truly are.

Follow @gela.flow
P

Prince

I started dancing because it made me feel alive — the rhythm, energy and expression came together like magic. What began as a hobby became a lifestyle and a way to inspire others through movement.

My foundation is Hip-Hop — raw, expressive and full of groove — and I love blending street and freestyle elements to create something authentic and powerful.

In my classes, expect energy, connection and growth. We drill musicality, foundation and confidence so you leave feeling stronger and more in tune with the music.

My teaching philosophy: dance isn’t just steps — it’s how it feels. I’m here to push limits, build community and create a space where everyone can express themselves freely.

Advice for dancers: stay consistent. Don’t chase perfection — chase progress. Every move, class and mistake is part of your journey. Keep the passion alive and never stop learning.

Follow @_prince_.chauhan
